About Us

Mechapex Technologies LLP started with a vision to serve the design departments of Automotive, Defense, Agriculture, Farm Industry, and Civil Structural design works. We have grown from a small startup to a trusted partner for tier-1 companies, offering comprehensive engineering solutions and HR consultancy services.

At Mechapex, we believe: "If you have the energy to conceive new ideas, utilize that energy in opening up fresh avenues in the area of system engineering and build of new products."
